Overview

"The Last Kingdom" is a historical drama television series based on Bernard Cornwell's Saxon Stories series of novels. The series follows the life of Uhtred of Bebbanburg, a Saxon noble who is captured and raised by Danes.

Main Characters

Uhtred of Bebbanburg - Uhtred, the protagonist, embodies the clash of Saxon and Viking cultures. Born a Saxon noble, he is captured and raised by Danes, navigating the challenges of dual identity. Skilled in combat and torn between loyalties, Uhtred's quest for belonging and his ancestral rights adds depth to the series.

Alfred the Great - King of Wessex, Alfred is a pivotal figure in the resistance against Viking invasions. A visionary leader and scholar, he strives to unite the fractured kingdoms into a single England. Alfred's strategic brilliance and unwavering commitment to learning shape the destiny of the Saxon lands.

Brida - Brida, Uhtred's childhood friend, undergoes a transformation from Saxon villager to formidable warrior. Fierce and independent, Brida navigates her own path amidst the chaos of Viking raids and Saxon conflicts. Her complex relationship with Uhtred adds emotional depth to the narrative.

"The Last Kingdom" is a riveting historical drama that unfolds against the backdrop of the Viking invasion of England. Adapted from Bernard Cornwell's "Saxon Stories" novels, the series revolves around Uhtred of Bebbanburg, a Saxon noble raised by Vikings. Imbued with political intrigue, battles, and complex character relationships, the show masterfully explores the cultural clash between Saxons and Danes during a tumultuous period in English history. Uhtred's journey, torn between loyalties and seeking his true identity, adds a compelling layer to this captivating tale of survival and power struggles in a land divided.
